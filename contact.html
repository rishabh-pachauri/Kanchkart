Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, simply return it within 30 days for a full refund.

How long does shipping take?

Standard delivery takes 2-3 business days within India. We also offer express shipping for urgent orders.

Is your glass dishwasher safe?

Yes! All our glass products are dishwasher safe (top rack recommended). They're also microwave and oven-safe up to 120°C.

Absolutely! For bulk orders and corporate gifting, please email bulk@kanchkart.com or call us at +91 98765 43211.

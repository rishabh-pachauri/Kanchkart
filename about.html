Our Story

Our Mission

At KanchKart, our mission is simple yet powerful: to promote healthy and sustainable living through premium glass products.

We believe that every Indian household deserves access to high-quality glass alternatives to plastic. We're not just selling products; we're selling a lifestyle choice that's better for your health, your family, and our planet.

Our vision is to replace a billion plastic bottles with beautiful, durable glass alternatives, making a tangible difference in reducing plastic pollution while improving the quality of life for millions.

Our Journey

From idea to impact

2021

The Beginning

A group of friends noticed the plastic pollution crisis and decided to do something about it. KanchKart was born.

2022

First Products Launch

We launched our premium water bottles and mason jars. The response from the market was overwhelming.

2023

Expanded Collection

Added beer mugs, storage containers, and specialized drinkware to our growing product line.

2024

Going Global

Now serving customers across India with plans to expand internationally. Our impact grows every day.

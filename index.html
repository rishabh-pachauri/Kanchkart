Our Story

How KanchKart Began

KanchKart was founded with a simple mission: to make premium glass products accessible to every Indian household. We noticed that while people were increasingly concerned about plastic pollution and health, quality glass alternatives were either unavailable or prohibitively expensive.

We decided to change that. Our founders spent months researching, testing, and perfecting glass products that combine:

  • Premium Quality: Sourced from the finest glass manufacturers
  • Affordable Pricing: Direct-to-consumer model eliminates middlemen
  • Beautiful Design: Products that look as good as they function
  • Environmental Impact: Every purchase supports a cleaner planet

Today, KanchKart is on a mission to replace a billion plastic bottles with premium glass alternatives. Join us in choosing glass, choosing health, and choosing a better future.
